Potions

Minor Health Potion This potion is generally used for a quick top-up on health or for a quick, emergency heal.
The imbiber will heal for 9 hit points (HP).

Moderate Health Potion If you took a beating during a battle, this potion is for you.
Drinking it will heal you for 15 hit points (HP).

Major Health Potion This potion will save you from the brink of death.
You will gain 25 hit points (HP) from drinking it.

Potion of Invisibility For those who don't want to be seen - for good or for ill - this potion will turn you invisible for 8 hours.
Any attack action or spell casting will break the effect; moving, opening doors, and similar actions will not.
